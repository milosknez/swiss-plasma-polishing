Who we are

Precision finishing, made in Switzerland

We are a Swiss deep-tech company bringing patented plasma polishing to industries that demand a flawless, repeatable surface finish.

Our story

Born from Swiss research

Swiss Plasma Polishing grew out of years of applied research into cleaner, faster metal finishing. We set out to replace harsh, multi-step chemical polishing with a single water-based plasma process that is safe for people and the environment. Today we finish parts for medical, semiconductor, food and industrial customers across Europe.
