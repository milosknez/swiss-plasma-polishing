The technology

A revolutionary way to finish metal

Plasma polishing has evolved into a revolutionary technology for achieving incredibly smooth, high-gloss metal surfaces. For our customers, that means parts with a flawless, best-in-class finish, quickly and cleanly. Here is how it works, and the value it brings to your products.

The approach

A cleaner, smarter approach

Today, polishing requires multiple steps to achieve the desired surface roughness. It involves manual polishing and electrochemical polishing, which uses harsh, toxic acids that are harmful to people and the environment. Plasma polishing takes a much cleaner, smarter approach.

Bath process

Plasma Electrolytic Polishing

During Plasma Electrolytic Polishing, the metal part is submerged in an environmentally friendly, water-based salt solution and connected to an electrical power source. This creates a thin-layer plasma around the entire surface of the part. This plasma removes all peaks and imperfections on the surface, resulting in a smooth, polished surface.

Jet process

Jet Plasma Polishing

Taking this a step further is Jet Plasma Polishing. Instead of submerging the part in a bath, a specialised nozzle sprays a targeted stream, or jet, of the liquid solution directly onto the specific area that needs finishing. The electrical current travels right through this liquid stream, creating the polishing plasma exactly where the jet touches the metal.

Applications

Where plasma polishing fits

From large surfaces to inner contours, on conventional and 3D printed parts.

01

Large surfaces

With PEP-Jet we polish large areas that are not possible in electrochemical or plasma electrolytic baths.

02

Local polishing and geometry preservation

The electrolyte jet polishes specific areas precisely, so geometries and corners stay sharp.

03

Inner contours and cavities

With the jet and selective polishing we reach areas that mechanical methods and electropolishing cannot.

04

3D printed surfaces

With PEP we polish rough printed surfaces directly to a fine finish, with no precleaning or preprocessing.
